# Outreach Intake Template

What to collect from a prospect when they request a sample brief. Mirrors the
"What to include in your email" block on the site so replies are easy to action.

## Ask the prospect for

1. **Company / ticker** — the public company or symbol they want covered.
2. **Source link** — the filing, earnings report, 10-K, or transcript (URL or attachment).
3. **Desired output** — newsletter section, X thread, or full research brief.
4. **Deadline** — when they need it back.
5. **Their channel** — publication, newsletter name, or social handle (and rough audience size).

## Nice-to-have (don't block on these)

- Preferred length / format (bullet brief vs prose).
- Tone (neutral analyst vs punchy creator voice).
- Any specific angle or question they already have in mind.

## Intake record (copy per prospect)

```
Name:
Email:
Channel / audience:
Company / ticker:
Source link:
Desired output:
Deadline:
Notes:
Status: [new / brief sent / replied / passed]
```

## Reply template (paste into email)

> Thanks for reaching out. To turn this around quickly, can you confirm:
> 1) company/ticker, 2) the exact filing or transcript link, 3) the output you want
> (newsletter section / X thread / full brief), 4) your deadline, and 5) where it will run.
> I'll send back a source-backed brief you can publish from. Research content only, not financial advice.
